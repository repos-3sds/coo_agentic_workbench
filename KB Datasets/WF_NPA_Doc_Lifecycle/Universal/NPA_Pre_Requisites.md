# NPA Pre-Requisites - Pre-Checks Before Initiating an NPA

**Version**: 1.0
**Effective Date**: February 2026
**Purpose**: Define mandatory pre-checks and prerequisites before initiating any New Product Approval process

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document outlines the comprehensive pre-requisite framework that must be completed before initiating any New Product Approval (NPA) submission. These pre-checks ensure NPA readiness, prevent incomplete submissions, and accelerate the approval process through proper preparation.

---

## 1. STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT PRE-CHECKS

### 1.1 Business Case Validation
**Status**: ✅ MANDATORY for ALL NPAs

#### Pre-Check Requirements:
- [ ] **Strategic Alignment Assessment**
  - Product aligns with MBS strategic priorities
  - Supports digital transformation objectives
  - Contributes to customer experience enhancement
  - Fits within approved business unit mandates

- [ ] **Market Opportunity Validation**
  - Market research completed and documented
  - Competitive analysis performed
  - Customer demand validated through surveys/research
  - Revenue projections supported by data

- [ ] **Business Case Approval**
  - Financial projections prepared (3-year minimum)
  - ROI analysis completed
  - Cost-benefit analysis documented
  - Senior management approval obtained

**Validation Checklist:**
```
□ Business case document prepared and approved
□ Financial projections validated by Finance team
□ Market research data available
□ Strategic alignment confirmed by Group Product Head
□ Resource allocation approved
□ Budget approval obtained
```

## 2. CLASSIFICATION PRE-DETERMINATION

### 2.1 NPA Classification Assessment
**Status**: ✅ MANDATORY - Must be completed before NPA initiation

#### Classification Decision Tree:

```
START → Is this product completely new to MBS Group?
├── YES → NEW-TO-GROUP (NTG)
│   ├── Criteria Met: >50% of NTG indicators
│   ├── Process: Full NPA Required
│   └── Timeline: 12-16 weeks
│
└── NO → Is this a modification of existing product?
    ├── YES → VARIATION
    │   ├── Material Change → Full NPA
    │   └── Minor Change → NPA Lite eligible
    │
    └── NO → EXISTING
        ├── Different market/customer → NPA Lite eligible
        └── Identical implementation → Evergreen eligible
```

#### NTG Classification Criteria (20 Indicators):
- [ ] **Market Innovation**: Novel market approach or customer segment
- [ ] **Product Structure**: Fundamentally new product architecture
- [ ] **Technology Platform**: Requires new technology infrastructure
- [ ] **Risk Profile**: Introduces new risk categories
- [ ] **Regulatory Framework**: Subject to new regulatory requirements
- [ ] **Operational Model**: Requires new operational processes
- [ ] **Revenue Model**: Introduces new revenue streams
- [ ] **Partnership Requirements**: Requires new external partnerships
- [ ] **Capital Treatment**: New capital allocation methodology
- [ ] **Legal Documentation**: Requires new legal agreements
- [ ] **Compliance Framework**: New compliance monitoring needed
- [ ] **Market Making**: Bank takes new market-making roles
- [ ] **Currency Exposure**: Involves new currency exposures
- [ ] **Settlement Mechanism**: Uses new settlement methods
- [ ] **Counterparty Types**: Engages new counterparty categories
- [ ] **Geographic Scope**: Enters new geographic markets
- [ ] **Customer Segment**: Targets previously unserved segments
- [ ] **Distribution Channel**: Uses new distribution mechanisms
- [ ] **Pricing Model**: Implements novel pricing strategies
- [ ] **Data Requirements**: Needs new data management approaches

**Classification Validation:**
```
□ All 20 NTG criteria assessed
□ Classification decision documented with rationale
□ Senior stakeholder agreement on classification
□ Process type determined (Full NPA/NPA Lite/Bundling/Evergreen)
□ Expected timeline communicated to stakeholders
```

### 2.2 Process Type Selection
**Status**: ✅ MANDATORY - Determines approval workflow

#### Process Selection Matrix:

| Classification | Material Change | Complexity | Recommended Process |
|---------------|-----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| **New-to-Group** | N/A | High | Full NPA |
| **Variation** | Yes | Medium-High | Full NPA |
| **Variation** | No | Low-Medium | NPA Lite |
| **Existing** | Minimal | Low | Bundling/Evergreen |

#### Additional Process Triggers:
- [ ] **PAC Approval Required**: Any NTG product
- [ ] **Bundling Process**: Combination of multiple existing products
- [ ] **Evergreen Eligible**: Standard products with pre-approved parameters
- [ ] **Full NPA Override**: High-risk variations despite minor changes

## 11. PRE-REQUISITE FAILURE PROTOCOLS

### 11.1 Insufficient Readiness Response
**When Overall Readiness Score < 85%:**

#### Mandatory Actions:
1. **Gap Analysis**: Detailed assessment of readiness shortfalls
2. **Remediation Plan**: Time-bound action plan to address gaps
3. **Stakeholder Communication**: Notify all parties of delay
4. **Timeline Revision**: Adjust NPA initiation timeline
5. **Resource Reallocation**: Redirect resources to address gaps
6. **Re-Assessment**: Complete readiness review after remediation

#### Escalation Procedures:
- **Group Product Head** notification within 24 hours
- **Group COO** briefing within 48 hours
- **Senior stakeholder** communication within 1 week
- **Revised timeline** communication to all parties

### 11.2 Critical Path Failure Response
**When Critical Items Incomplete:**

#### Immediate Actions:
- **NPA Process Suspension**: Formal halt to NPA initiation
- **Senior Management Escalation**: Immediate notification to Group COO
- **Risk Assessment**: Evaluation of delay impact
- **Alternative Strategy**: Assessment of alternative approaches
- **Resource Mobilization**: Emergency resource allocation if needed
