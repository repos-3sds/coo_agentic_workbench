# NPA Documents Required - Document Requirements by NPA Type

**Version**: 1.0
**Effective Date**: February 2026
**Purpose**: Define document requirements for different types of NPA submissions

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document provides a comprehensive mapping of document requirements based on NPA classification, process type, and product characteristics. It serves as a definitive reference for document preparation and submission requirements across all NPA categories.

---

## 1. DOCUMENT FRAMEWORK OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Document Classification System

#### Document Categories:
- **🟢 Core Documents**: Required for ALL NPAs
- **🟡 Conditional Documents**: Required based on specific criteria
- **🟠 Supplementary Documents**: Enhance submission quality
- **🔴 Critical Path Documents**: Block approval if missing

#### Document Sources:
- **Auto-Generated**: System-produced from templates
- **Business Prepared**: Created by proposing business unit
- **External**: Third-party provided (legal opinions, audits, etc.)
- **Regulatory**: Official regulatory submissions/approvals

### 1.2 Document Readiness Matrix

| NPA Type | Core Docs | Conditional Docs | Total Documents | Preparation Time |
|----------|-----------|------------------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Full NPA (NTG)** | 25 | 15-20 | 40-45 | 8-12 weeks |
| **Full NPA (Variation)** | 22 | 10-15 | 32-37 | 6-8 weeks |
| **NPA Lite** | 18 | 5-10 | 23-28 | 4-6 weeks |
| **Bundling** | 20 | 8-12 | 28-32 | 5-7 weeks |
| **Evergreen** | 12 | 3-5 | 15-17 | 2-3 weeks |

## 8. DOCUMENT QUALITY AND VALIDATION

### 8.1 Document Quality Standards

#### Quality Framework:
- **Completeness**: All required sections filled
- **Accuracy**: Information verified and validated
- **Consistency**: Alignment across all documents
- **Clarity**: Clear and understandable presentation
- **Compliance**: Adherence to regulatory standards

#### Quality Assurance Checklist:
```
□ All mandatory documents present
□ Document versions current and consistent
□ Cross-references validated
□ Regulatory requirements addressed
□ Risk assessments comprehensive
□ Financial projections realistic
□ Legal documentation complete
□ External approvals obtained
□ Sign-off requirements met
□ Implementation readiness confirmed
```

### 8.2 Document Validation Process

#### Validation Stages:
1. **Automated Validation**: System checks for completeness and format
2. **Business Validation**: Content review by business units
3. **Risk Validation**: Risk assessment adequacy review
4. **Compliance Validation**: Regulatory compliance verification
5. **Legal Validation**: Legal documentation adequacy
6. **Final Validation**: Senior management sign-off

#### Validation Criteria:
| Validation Type | Pass Threshold | Review Authority | Timeline |
|----------------|----------------|------------------|----------|
| **Automated** | 100% completeness | System | Real-time |
| **Business** | Content adequacy | Business Head | 5 days |
| **Risk** | Risk coverage | CRO/Head of Risk | 7 days |
| **Compliance** | Regulatory alignment | CCO | 7 days |
| **Legal** | Legal adequacy | General Counsel | 5 days |
| **Final** | Overall readiness | Group COO | 3 days |

## 11. DOCUMENT PREPARATION TIMELINE

### 11.1 Standard Preparation Timeline

#### Full NPA (New-to-Group):
```
Week 1-2: Core document template setup
Week 3-4: Business case and specifications
Week 5-6: Risk and compliance assessments
Week 7-8: Technical and operational documentation
Week 9-10: External document procurement
Week 11-12: Quality assurance and validation
```

#### NPA Lite:
```
Week 1: Core document template setup
Week 2-3: Simplified specifications and risk assessment
Week 4-5: Supporting documentation
Week 6: Quality assurance and validation
```

#### Bundling:
```
Week 1-2: Product combination analysis
Week 3-4: Cross-product risk assessment
Week 5-6: Revenue allocation and operational planning
Week 7: Validation and submission
```

### 11.2 Critical Path Management

#### Document Dependencies:
- **Business Case** → Risk Assessment → Technical Specifications
- **Legal Documentation** → Compliance Assessment → Regulatory Submissions
- **External Opinions** → Internal Risk Validation → Final Approval
- **System Documentation** → Operational Procedures → Implementation Planning

#### Bottleneck Management:
- **External Documents**: Start procurement early (Week 1)
- **Regulatory Approvals**: Initiate pre-consultation
- **Legal Opinions**: Engage counsel at project start
- **Model Validation**: Begin parallel to NPA preparation
